Address

8300 Camino Del Oro, La Jolla, CA 92037

The beach does not have a closing time, so you can go whenever you want or can go.

Directions

Get on CA-56 W/State Hwy 56 W from Carmel Valley Rd. Continue on CA-56 W/State Hwy 56 W. Take I-5 S to La Jolla Village Dr. Take exit 28 from I-5 S. Continue on La Jolla Village Dr. Take Prestwick Dr and Calle Del Oro to Camino Del Oro.

Parking

Parking here at the becah is extremely limited compared to how many people visit. It is highly suggested to park .5 miles away and walk to the beach in order to avoid the wait; however, it takes one 20-30 miutes on average to find a spot, so if you are willing to wait or feel lucky there is always a chance.
