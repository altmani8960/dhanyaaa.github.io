A Brief History

It is suggested that the Kumeyaay name for the area was transcribed by the Spanish settlers as La Jolla. An alternative, pseudo-etymological suggestion for the origin of the name is that it is an alternate spelling of the Spanish word la joya, which means "the jewel".

About

This beach is a well known beach, here in San Diego. The beach provides visitors a guarentee of a good time. La Jolla Shores is a place for everyone from people that need a place to read a book to people that want to surf the beautiful waves here.
